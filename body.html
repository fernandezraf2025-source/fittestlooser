BODY TYPES

Know your body, then build discipline around it.

Ectomorph

Lean body type with fast metabolism.

Challenge: Hard to gain muscle

Focus: Calorie surplus + heavy compound lifts

Best workouts: Squats, deadlifts, bench press

Mesomorph

Athletic build, naturally muscular.

Advantage: Gains muscle fast

Focus: Progressive overload

Best workouts: Balanced push/pull/legs split

Endomorph

Stores fat easily, slower metabolism.

Challenge: Fat loss difficulty

Focus: Calorie control + cardio

Best workouts: HIIT + full body training

IMPORTANT REMINDER

Your body type is not your limit. It is just your starting point.

Discipline > Genetics. Consistency always wins.
